About Me

Supriya Sanjay is a rising junior at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive Brain Sciences.

She has had experience as a research assistant in a social cognition lab and has worked as a teaching assistant for

an Introductory Computer Science course. She enjoys singing and is a part of an Acapella group at Tufts. She also is a

peer leader on Team Q, the Tufts LGBT Center's peer mentorship program.
